Information & Resume

The tl;dr Verison

I have 14 years of print design experience, 11 of which were spent at the Milwaukee Journal Sentinel, where I designed anything from 1A to Business covers to Arts & Entertainment covers and graphics.

I have also been designing web sites for 16 years – for fun, until a few years ago.

I started my undergraduate education at EAI Tech, in Sophia-Antipolis, France (where I grew up) before transferring to the University of South Carolina’s School of Journalism and Mass Communications, where I earned my Bachelor’s degree.

I then jumped into the Missouri School of Journalism’s graduate program where I focused on design and earned my Masters in 2004.

In addition, I have an associate degree in Web Design from the Milwaukee Area Technical College and have finished a web development bootcamp course in Chicago at Anyone Can Learn To Code (now renamed Actualize).
